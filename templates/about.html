How to Use

Welcome to our e-commerce application! Here's a quick guide on how to use it:

  • Browse Products: Visit the "Store" section to browse through our wide range of products.
  • Add to Cart: Found something you like? Simply click on the product to view details and add it to your shopping cart.
  • Checkout: Once you've added all the items you want, proceed to the checkout page to complete your purchase.
  • User Account: Create an account to manage your orders, track shipments, and save your preferences.
  • Payment: We offer various payment methods for your convenience, including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and more.
